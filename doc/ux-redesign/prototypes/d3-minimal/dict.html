Monier-Williams Sanskrit-English Dictionary · MW

Compiled by Sir Monier Monier-Williams and published at Oxford in 1899, this is the most widely used Sanskrit–English dictionary. It was digitized by the Cologne Digital Sanskrit Dictionaries project; the current Cologne edition dates from 2020 (previous edition 2014).
