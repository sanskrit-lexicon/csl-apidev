Sir Monier Monier-Williams' A Sanskrit-English Dictionary, published at Oxford in 1899, remains the most widely consulted Sanskrit–English reference work. It was digitized by the Cologne Digital Sanskrit Dictionaries project, which links every entry back to the scanned pages of the printed edition.
