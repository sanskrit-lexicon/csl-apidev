PWKVN display experiments

Side-by-side rendering experiments for the Böhtlingk dictionary family with Richard Schmidt's Nachträge. Each display lays out the same headword across a different combination of source columns.

pwg
Großes Petersburger Wörterbuch (Böhtlingk & Roth)
pwk
Petersburger Wörterbuch, kürzere Fassung (Böhtlingk)
pwkvn
experimental combined pwk display (this repo)
sch
Schmidt, Nachträge zum Sanskrit-Wörterbuch

Displays

  1. 01 pwk | sch + pwkvn →
  2. 02 pwg + pwk | pwkvn + sch →
  3. 03 pwg | pwk | pwkvn + sch →

Minimal-direction mockup (H563) of the real pwkvn/index.html landing — content unchanged, restyle only. The three experiments load live data from the Cologne server. Citation-suggestion search runs over headwords present in either Schmidt or pwkvn.
